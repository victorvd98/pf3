Victor Van Damme — Work Archive — McDo on your mind?

Everybody craves a burger from time to time.

A bold billboard campaign with stopping power where the top of models’ heads transformed into irresistible burgers. A playful, eye-catching way to make McDonald’s cravings impossible to ignore.

By visually putting McDonald’s front and center — or, well, on top — we sparked cravings and kept the brand top of mind, wherever people went.
