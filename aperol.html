Victor Van Damme — Work Archive — The Aperol Spritz Band

Not your average brand zone. Not your average band.

At packed summer festivals, Aperol didn’t want to be just another sponsor — we wanted to be a moment. So we created a supergroup that captured the essence of the drink itself: unexpected ingredients, mixed to perfection.

The Aperol Spritz Band brought together artists from wildly different genres for one-off jam sessions. No setlists. No repeats. Just pure, playful spontaneity — just like the perfect Aperol Spritz.
